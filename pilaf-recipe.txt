Ingredients:

- oil
- meat
- onion
- carrots
- rice
- salt

Directions:

1. Heat oil.
2. Add meat and cook about 5 mins, stirring.
3. Add onions and cook about 10 mins, stirring.
4. Add carrots, reduce heat, and cook for about 10 mins
5. Add rice, add hot water, add salt, increase heat, don't stir, cook until water not visible.
6. Reduce heat, put lid, cook for about 20 mins, occasionally letting the steam out.
